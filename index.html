What We Do

Our Services

Every service is delivered with the precision, attention to detail and care that the Prestige name stands for.

01

Classic Haircut

A timeless haircut tailored to your face shape, hair type and personal style. Includes consultation, wash, cut and finish.

02

Skin Fade

Our signature skin fade is executed with precision clipper work graduating seamlessly from skin to your chosen length. A true art form.

03

Beard Trim & Shape

Expert beard grooming using scissors and razor to create the perfect shape and definition for your beard style.

Hot Towel Shave

The classic straight razor shave with hot towel preparation, premium shaving cream and post-shave treatment for the ultimate experience.

Cut & Beard Combo

Kids Cut

A relaxed and friendly haircut experience for boys up to 14 years old. Patient barbers who make kids feel comfortable every time.

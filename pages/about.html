Our Story

Craft, Character
& Precision

Prestige was founded on one belief — that a great barbershop should be more than just a place you get your hair cut. It should be a place you actually want to come back to.

How It Started

Built by barbers,
for their clients

Prestige Barbershop was founded in 2015 by master barber Liam Doyle after 12 years cutting hair in some of Dublin's most respected shops. Liam had a vision for something different — a space that combined old school barbering craft with a modern, premium experience.

The result was Prestige. A barbershop that takes the craft seriously, invests in the best equipment and products and employs only barbers who share the same commitment to quality.

Ten years later we've built a loyal community of clients who come to us not just for a haircut but for the whole experience — the atmosphere, the conversation and the confidence that comes with knowing you're in expert hands.
